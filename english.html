Test E4

What is the E4 test?

The E4 test (Design and Maintenance of IT solutions) is a BTS SIO test which aims to assess the skills acquired by the candidate. Several skills from the Framework are validated:
  • Compliance and operationality of the proposed solution
  • Relevance of the choice of components and reliability of their implementation.
  • Rigor in execution
  • Efficiency in the implementation of the environment mobilized for production and the methods chosen
  • Relevance of the critical analysis of the results obtained and the approach followe
  • Quality and effectiveness of professional communication implemented

The test is accompanied by a summary table which provides all of the skills that must be achieved during the projects presented.
